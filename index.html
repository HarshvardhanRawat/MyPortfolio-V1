About Me

Who am I?

I'm a passionate B.Tech CSD student, a self-driven developer, and a photographer & storyteller. Whether it's writing clean code, capturing raw moments, or crafting aesthetic visuals, I love blending creativity with purpose.

I’ve had the privilege of contributing to reputed platforms like Legatus Youth Conferences (LYC), where I worked my way from delegate to Head of Photography, leading a team of 55+ creatives. My lens has captured not just events but impact — and that same mindset reflects in the way I build and design digital experiences.
